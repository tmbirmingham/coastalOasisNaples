Welcome to Coastal Oasis — Where Every Day Ends in a Sunset — a brand-new luxury residence at The Residences at Naples Beach Club, where refined coastal design, sweeping southwest Gulf views, and effortless turnkey living create an extraordinary Naples lifestyle. Positioned on the third residential floor (fourth building level), this exceptional residence offers 3,440 square feet of beautifully designed living space, featuring 3 bedrooms, 3 full bathrooms, and a private powder bath. Expansive glass doors and windows invite natural light throughout, while framing captivating southwest Gulf views that deliver spectacular sunsets throughout Naples' coveted winter season. Designed in a timeless beach-inspired coastal style, the interiors reflect relaxed elegance and are offered fully furnished, allowing immediate enjoyment without the time or expense of design and furnishing. Coastal Oasis provides a seamless, move-in-ready opportunity rarely found in newly completed luxury residences. The beautifully designed kitchen blends style and functionality, thoughtfully curated for elevated entertaining and effortless everyday living. Premium integrated appliances include a dramatic 72-inch Sub-Zero refrigerator/freezer, a 48-inch Wolf gas range with pot filler and custom hood, Wolf double convection wall ovens with warming drawer, microwave and steam ovens, and dual side-by-side Miele integrated dishwashers. The adjacent wet bar, complete with Sub-Zero wine refrigeration, enhances the home’s entertaining appeal, while dual stacked Asko washer and dryer sets provide added convenience for effortless coastal living. The spacious 27-foot wide private terrace, spanning nearly 250 square feet, extends the living experience outdoors — an ideal setting to relax with morning coffee, entertain guests, or unwind as the sun sets over the Gulf. Located within the prestigious Residences at Naples Beach Club, residents enjoy access to one of Naples’ most celebrated beachfront destinations, blending luxury living with resort-inspired surroundings. In addition, Four Seasons concierge level service delivers an unparalleled standard of personalized attention and convenience. Whether serving as a seasonal retreat or year-round residence, Coastal Oasis offers an exceptional opportunity to own a newly completed Gulf-view home where every evening delivers a breathtaking sunset.
